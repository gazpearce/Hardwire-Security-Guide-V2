Does using wireless security cameras expose your home network to deauthentication attacks?

Authoritative Engineering Guide • Newcastle, Durham, Sunderland & Middlesbrough

Controversial Verdict: Wireless alarm and camera systems (like SimpliSafe) are inherently insecure and easily defeated by £20 RF jammers or deauthentication scripts run from a laptop. Relying on wireless signals for home defense is a technical joke compared to hardwired PoE configurations.

Technical Specifications & Implementation
